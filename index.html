Mad Libs

Create your own mad lib. You can choose up to 4 Nouns, 4 Adjectives, 4 Adverbs, and 4 Verbs. You will need to put in a comma(,) after each word to seperate each word. If your choose less then 4 words on each a random word will be generated for you or if you want to get crazy just leave it blank to generate a radome mad lib.
